Operating cycle

How a club month works

1. Selection

Pick the buffet worth documenting

The stop has to be worthy of a club outing, not just convenient.

2. Attendance

Show up hungry and on purpose

The club is built on actual turnout, not hypothetical enthusiasm.

3. Weigh-in

Record the before and after honestly

The numbers are the point. We keep them because the ritual means more when it is measured.

4. Archive

Preserve the visit

Each trip becomes part of the permanent ledger: restaurant, month, year, attendance, and total gain.
