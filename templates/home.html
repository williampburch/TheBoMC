About the club

What The Buffet of the Month Club actually does

Monthly ritual

One buffet, one log entry

The club picks a buffet, shows up hungry, and records the visit as a single monthly event in the ledger.

Weigh-ins

Before and after

Members who attend can log a pre-buffet and post-buffet weight so the club can track the damage honestly.

Club history

Archive and commentary

Signed-in members can browse the trip archive, review the leaderboards, and add to the running club comment thread.

Admin role

Keep the ledger clean

Admins manage visits, maintain member accounts, reset passwords when needed, and grant admin access to the right people.
